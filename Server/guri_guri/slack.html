What is this minc bot?

minc helps you understand what is happening within your Slack channels. It shows you what is popular at the moment and how your team is feeling.

You can see which items are currently 'hot' by evaluating reactions of messages and get a summary of the sentiment by looking at the positivity of individual messages on certain dates or over a longer time period.


Let's go install it

First step is to add the minc bot to your Slack team. For that click the below button and follow the process. (By adding minc to Slack you accept our Privacy Policy.)

Then all you have to do is write a direct message to your new minc bot, or add minc to a channel and the show can begin.


Getting in the mood

minc will tell you how positive the messages have been on certain days or over a period. For example ​you can ask What was the mood in channel #social yesterday?, ​mood #everbody last week?​ or ​#random mood 04/13/2016 - 04/29/2016.

As a return message you will get a percentage, which represents the number of positive messages as part of the overall messages:

In the background we take at each message in that timeframe, analyse the sentiment for it and give you the summary.


It is getting hot

minc will tell you which items have most reactions attached to them relatively to their age. For this we look at all reactions regardless if positive or negative. We want to show you what new items are mostly discussed (in particular reactions divided by the age to the gravityth power). For example ​What is hot in #social? or #random hot.

In return you will get 5 posts which currently have the highest hot score:

Clicking on individual reply posts will send you to the original message.
